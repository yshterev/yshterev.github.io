About

Chicagoland’s vending leader.

For over 50 years, Mark Vend has provided clients throughout Chicago with food and beverage programs to meet their unique needs.

We’re big enough to offer the latest products and technology but small enough that we get to know you, your culture and your concerns. It’s why so many of Chicago’s leading companies have stayed with us for so long.

Mark Vend offers:

  • Custom programs for any size organization, from 50 employees to more than 10,000
  • The widest selection of snacks, beverages and fresh items available
  • State-of-the-art technology, with every machine networked and monitored, and a web portal for online ordering and reports
  • A forward-thinking mindset — we’re constantly exploring new products and services to bring you
  • The most attentive customer service in the industry, bar none — it’s what sets us apart.
History

Chicagoland’s vending leader.

We’ve lived and breathed this business since 1962. That’s when Andy and Bonnie Stein opened Mark Vend on Chicago’s North Shore with one truck, 12 vending machines and a dream.

Today, Mark Vend has a fleet of 35 vehicles, a full-time staff of 90 and a 75,000-square-foot headquarters facility — all dedicated to managing coffee, micro market, pantry and vending at over 300 locations across Chicagoland.

Building on their parents’ legacy, owners Mark and Daniel Stein are committed to serving their clients better than anyone else in the industry. Nothing makes them happier than talking vending.
