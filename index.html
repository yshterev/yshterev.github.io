DINING

A seamless experience.

Gourmet coffee and espresso. A pantry stocked with loads of options. Everything’s fresh. Everyone’s happy. That’s the experience Mark Vend delivers. Most of the time, you won’t even know we’ve been there.

Our singular focus is creating the ideal food and beverage program for your unique needs. No hassles, no worries, no effort. That’s why Chicago’s best companies and institutions choose Mark Vend.

We deliver what’s trending.

Filtered water

Take your meetings up a notch with this elegant, eco-friendly alternative to bottled water. Vivreau dispenses cold filtered water, still or sparkling.

Fountain drinks

The Coca-Cola Freestyle serves up 125+ beverage options — far more than you can offer in bottles and cans. Besides a happy staff, this modern-day soda fountain means less recycling and less waste.

Cold brew coffee

Increasingly popular with java lovers, cold brew coffee on tap is popping up in workplaces everywhere. We set you up with a kegerator and keep it filled with favorite brands like Stumptown.

Specialized vending

Think beyond soda and chips. Today’s vending machines can dispense frozen meals, fresh salads, school supplies, bicycle parts, even company-branded merchandise. Just about anything is possible.

Catered meals

If it involves feeding your team, we can do it: pop-up eateries for in-house lunch events, personal chef service, restaurant meal delivery with revolving daily menus…you name it!

Proudly serving Chicago’s best companies since 1962.
